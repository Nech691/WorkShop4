To create a scrapbook art:                                                      1. Use the ''Background Colour'' button to select a new colour choice                                                                          2. Use the ''Reset'' buttton to confirm your choice               3. Generate some words using the ''Create Words'' bar and ''Generate'' button to set them randomly on the canvas       .... Each new word will re-randomise the location of all previous words as well                                                          4. Use the ''Change group of Images'' to randomly select between images of Cards, Flowers, Papers, & Statues          5. Click around the canvas to place new images from the randomly selected group                                                     .... Use the ''Change group of Images'' to randomly select a new group of images                                                            6. Pressing the "reset" button will reset the canvas background colour, group of words, and images displayed so you can start again
